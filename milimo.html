Common Questions

Frequently asked

How much does wedding car hire cost in Brisbane?+

milimo offers luxury wedding car hire packages tailored to your specific requirements. Pricing depends on vehicle selection (Bentley Continental, Genesis G80, Mercedes-Benz S-Class), number of vehicles, duration, and travel distance. We offer everything from single-vehicle packages to full fleet coordination for your entire bridal party. Call 1300 884 536 for a personalised quote.

How far in advance should I book a wedding car?+

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings, particularly during peak season (September to April). Popular vehicles like the Bentley Continental and Genesis G80 book up quickly. However, we always try to accommodate last-minute requests - call us to check availability.

Do you track flight arrivals for airport transfers?+

Yes. Our chauffeurs monitor flight arrivals in real-time and adjust pickup times accordingly. If your flight is delayed, we'll be there when you land - no extra charge. We provide door-to-door luxury airport transfers between Brisbane Airport and Brisbane CBD, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, and Northern NSW.

What areas does milimo service?+

milimo provides luxury limousine and chauffeur services across all of South East Queensland including Brisbane CBD, greater Brisbane, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, Toowoomba, and Northern NSW. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week with professional chauffeurs trained in defensive driving.
