This Week

Quick wins with immediate impact

These are the fixes that will make the biggest difference the fastest. No redesign needed - just targeted corrections to what's already there.

01
Fix the typos in your meta descriptions
Critical 10 min fix
Your homepage, fleet page, and blog page all have spelling errors in the meta descriptions that Google shows in search results. This kills credibility before anyone clicks.
What to fix:
Homepage: Mercesdes-Benz should be Mercedes-Benz
Fleet page: Merecdes and afforable should be Mercedes and affordable
Blog page: Coprorate should be Corporate, and is are available is broken grammar
02
Add your full address and Google Maps
Critical 15 min fix
Your contact page only shows "Geebung, Queensland" - no street address, no map, no business hours. Google needs this to put you in the local map pack.
Add to contact page: Full street address, business hours (24/7), embedded Google Map, and a link to your Google Business Profile.
03
Claim or optimise your Google Business Profile
Critical 30 min
Your Google Business Profile appears to be absent or poorly optimised. This is the single most important thing for a local service business. Competitors who show up in the Google Maps 3-pack get the clicks.
Action: Claim the profile at business.google.com, add all services, upload 20+ high-quality vehicle photos, add business hours, service areas, and start requesting Google reviews from recent clients.
04
Fix your page titles - stop repeating "Wedding Car Hire Brisbane"
High Impact 15 min fix
Your Fleet, Contact, and Reviews pages all have "Wedding Car Hire Brisbane" appended to the title. This confuses Google about what each page is actually about.
Each page needs its own keyword:
Fleet: Luxury Fleet - Bentley, Mercedes, Genesis | milimo Brisbane
Contact: Contact milimo Brisbane | 1300 884 536
Reviews: Customer Reviews | milimo Brisbane Limousine Service

05
Add LocalBusiness schema to every page
High Impact 30 min
Your site has basic schema from Yoast but is missing LocalBusiness structured data. This is what tells Google (and AI search engines) your exact location, services, hours, and service area.
Add JSON-LD: Business name, full address, geo coordinates, phone, opening hours, price range, service area (Brisbane, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast), aggregate rating from your reviews.
06
Add review schema to your testimonials
High Impact 20 min
You have 20+ glowing reviews on your site and 4.9 on Easy Weddings - but Google doesn't know about them because there's no Review schema. Adding this unlocks star ratings in search results.
Result: Gold stars appear next to your listing in Google search results. This dramatically increases click-through rate. Your competitors who have this are getting clicks you're missing.
07
Add FAQ sections to your top 3 service pages
High Impact 1-2 hours
This is the #1 thing missing for AI search visibility. When someone asks ChatGPT "how much does a wedding car cost in Brisbane?" - your site has no answer to extract. Adding FAQs with schema fixes this for both Google and AI.
Weddings page: How much does a wedding car cost? How far ahead should I book? Do you provide decorations? Can you do multiple trips?
Airport: How much is a limo to the Gold Coast? Do you track flight arrivals?
Corporate: Do you offer accounts? Can you handle conference logistics?
08
Collect fresh reviews (your latest is from 2021)
High Impact Ongoing
There's a 5-year gap in your on-site testimonials. Both Google and AI search engines treat freshness as a quality signal. A business with no reviews since 2021 looks like it might not be active anymore.
Quick approach: Email your 10 most recent clients and ask for a Google review. Add a review request link to your post-service email. Aim for 2-3 new Google reviews per month minimum.
