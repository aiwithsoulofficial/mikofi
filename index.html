Our Origin

From the highlands
of Papua New Guinea
to your cup

In the misty highlands above Port Moresby, where coffee trees descended from Jamaican Blue Mountain seeds have grown since the 1920s, smallholder farmers cultivate some of the world's most extraordinary Arabica beans.


miKofi partners directly with these farming communities - bringing their exceptional, naturally organic coffee to Queensland's finest cafes. Every bag supports local leaders, sustains highland communities, and delivers a flavour profile unlike anything else in the Australian market.

The Coffee

Single origin.
Highland grown.
Exceptional character.

PNG coffee carries a flavour profile that surprises even seasoned roasters - sweet, fruity, with a syrupy body that stands up beautifully in milk-based drinks.

Western Highlands

Waghi Valley Reserve

Mango Papaya Caramel

From the fertile Waghi Valley at 1,600m. Full-bodied with tropical fruit sweetness and a clean, lingering finish. Perfect as a house espresso.

Eastern Highlands

Goroka Gold

Dark Chocolate Honey Floral

Grown in the shadow of Mount Michael. Medium roast with rich chocolate undertones, honey sweetness, and delicate floral aromatics. A crowd favourite.

Simbu Province

Kundiawa Blend

Brown Sugar Citrus Walnut

A versatile blend from Simbu's steep mountain gardens. Balanced body with brown sugar sweetness and bright citrus lift. Exceptional in milk.

Our Impact

Every cup
builds a community

miKofi exists because great coffee should do more than taste good. Our supply chain is designed to keep value where it belongs - with the people who grow it.

Direct Community Partnerships

We work with local leaders in PNG's highland communities, ensuring fair returns reach the farmers and families who depend on the harvest.

Sustainable Supply Chain

Pre-roasted in origin, shipped direct to Queensland. Shorter distance than South American beans, supporting local PNG processing jobs.

Naturally Organic

PNG highland coffee grows without synthetic chemicals - not because of certification, but because that's simply how it's always been done.
