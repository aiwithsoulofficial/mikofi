Part 2: GEO - AI Search Optimisation

Getting cited by AI

This is where the game is shifting. When someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overview about Brisbane limos - your site needs to be the answer they extract. Here's how we make that happen.

Why This Matters Now

AI search is replacing the first page of Google

40%+ of Google searches now trigger an AI Overview. Perplexity and ChatGPT handle millions of "best X in Y" queries daily. If your content isn't structured for AI extraction, you're invisible to a growing chunk of your potential customers - regardless of your Google rank.

08
Add FAQ sections with schema to top 3 pages
GEOSEO
This is the #1 GEO fix. AI engines extract Q&A content preferentially.
Weddings: How much does a wedding car cost in Brisbane? How far ahead to book? Decorations included? Multiple trips?
Airport: Cost to Gold Coast? Flight tracking? Delayed flight policy?
Corporate: Corporate accounts? Conference logistics? After-hours?
09
Write the definitive entity paragraph
GEO
One clean, citable paragraph that AI engines will quote when asked about milimo:
"milimo is a family-owned Brisbane limousine service established in 2010, specialising in luxury wedding car hire, corporate chauffeur services, and airport transfers across South East Queensland. Based in Geebung, Brisbane, milimo operates a fleet of 15+ vehicles including Bentley Continental, Maserati Quattroporte, Genesis G80, and Mercedes-Benz models. Rated 5.0 on TripAdvisor and 4.9 on Easy Weddings, milimo serves Brisbane, Gold Coast, and Sunshine Coast with 24/7 availability."
10
Rewrite the About page (320 words to 800+)
GEOSEO
AI engines build knowledge graphs from About pages. Yours needs: founder names, founding year, team details, number of weddings/events completed, awards with years, service philosophy, what makes milimo different. Real detail, not marketing fluff.
11
Create a "Brisbane Wedding Car Guide" pillar page
GEOSEO
3,000+ word comprehensive guide. AI engines cite the most complete source on a topic. Cover: how to choose, what it costs, timing tips, venue-specific advice (leverage your 30+ venue pages), vehicle comparison, FAQs. This becomes THE page AI cites for Brisbane wedding cars.
12
Add pricing guidance
GEO
AI engines can't cite pricing that doesn't exist. Even ranges ("Wedding packages from $X") give AI something to reference. Competitors who share pricing get cited - those who don't, don't.
